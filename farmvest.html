FarmInvest.

Connect with farmers, Investors and marketers in the agriculture industry.

About

FarmInvest is a platform that connects farmers, investors and marketers. They are able to share their knowledge on the discussion threads, they can reach out to other, e.g a farmer can reach out to an investor and send him details about a farm or hectare he wants to cultivate.

The Problem

There are so many agrotech platforms where people can buy units of different farms like poultry or corn and wait a certain amount of time before collecting returns. But there has been a need for large scale investors who want to converse directly with the farmer or infact take a share in the business.

My Role

My role included research, ux strategy, information architecture, wireframing, visual design and prototyping

Design Process

User Research

I started my research by carrying out a survey, then we streamlined answers and interviewed a few people. From the research it was discovered that this platform could offer more. We found that we could have a better experience if we we had the home page as some sort of feed/community, where users can raise discussions/topics and have answers or people message them privately. This change was because we found from the research that a large percentage of people also really wanted to be more aware of the agricultural world.

User Map

Even though we are going to have various types of users, the user flow had to be the same, my goal was for everyone to have the same journey as not to distrupt the experience.

Wireframes

Creating wireframes, I started with mobile and then I did a desktop and also a tab vie, the reason behind this was because of web accessibility, during our research we discovered that mobile phones were more used than others, hence why I started with it and adapted the others to follow.

Visual Design

This stage was done after corrections had been made on the wireframes, the primary color used was green to depict agriculture.

Forum Feature

The home page serves as the feed, you automatically get recent discussions and trending investment opportunities, you are then able to select what discussion you want to be a part of, read through and even contribute. I designed it in such a way that even each reply in a discussion thread can be replied to.
