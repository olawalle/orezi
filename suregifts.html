FarmInvest.

A gift card ordering system, for employers to reward their employees

About

The Problem

Corporate organisations are looking for ways to reward their staf but have trouble iguring what the ideal gift should be because they don’t want to give them things they already own or cash.

The Solution

Suregifts business is a gift card ordering system, it allows employers to reward their employees using a voucher system instead of cash and in form of a gift card that can be used accross various stores.

My Role

My role included research, ux strategy, information architecture, wireframing, visual design and prototyping

Design Process

User Research

This product was already existing, my first step was to carefully analyze the existing web app. In addition to market research, I conducted user research of 5 people. 3 of whom where Human Resource managers in companies already using suregifts business and 2 were not aware of the product. (HR managers because they are mostly perfom gifting duties in an organization). From the data we were able to determine the needs of users. Some interview questions were:

  • Have you had troubles gifting? If yes, what were the issues you had?
  • What have store cards have you gifted?
  • Have you used Suregifts business to perform other tasks like paying electricity bill?
  • Have you had troubles gifting on Suregifts business? What are the troubles you currently have?
  • What payment method do you use the most on Suregifts business?

User Persona

Personas were created to not only help me understand our target audience, but to also help create a better product for them. With the realization that small factors like location, salary, and size of company can influence how those people make decisions, and in turn use our product.

User Map

The map used before hand worked, so what I had to do was edit the experience of each page and each feauture.

Wireframes

Wireframes were made and then I built prototypes for the website. Using paper first and then designing a low fidelity mockup of the apps to be tested with users.

Visual Design

The design was then created using the product color. The font was changed from the previous design

Send Voucher

There are three different methods of sending vouchers:

  • Single voucher

    This voucher is just for one person, you can customize the template of the gift card by adding pictures (of recipient) and then adding a message, it’s mostly used for anniversaries such as birthdays or wedding anniversaries. The gift vouchers can be sent via email or printed and hand delivered.

  • Group voucher

    In this situation, multiple recipients can be added and sent vouchers at once, t’s mostly used during festive periods or to gift a particular department, team or level of staff. In this type of voucher only one merchant can be used at a time and only a particular amount can be sent. (e.g 500 vouchers of ₦30,000 cafe neo vouchers)

  • Download voucher

    Vouchers such as phone line credits and internet subscription vouchers can be downloaded, printed and given to employees. They are good for gifting incentives immediately or spontaneously.

Paying Bills

Most bills payable in Nigeria can be made from this platform because it has a wallet and we wanted human resource managers to be able to do more than one function with their wallet. This way they can track company spend on bills from the order history, hence budgeting is easier and cost is saved.

Testing

Prototyping was done on marvel to determine how the screen lows from one to another and then given to users to test. Then the product went into production. After production there will be a live testing.

Conclusion

When doing user research I discovered that a lot of HR managers had difficulty using the system and called constantly for help. This was why I used a side bar menu because it is very familar and common and can easily be navigated.
