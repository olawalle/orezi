About me

I'm a designer of web and mobile experiences who is little too into simplicity and minimalism. I studied Architecture in the univeristy and the transition from designing spaces to products was a smooth one. I am more focused on the experience of the user, I believe that this translates in to good design. I just don't put screens together I do research to understand the user's goal of using the product and also the clients (owner of products) goals and then I design as a solution to bridge that gap whilst creating solutions for both parties.
